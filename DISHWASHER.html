Common Issues

Frequent dishwasher problems we handle

Dishwasher problems often begin with changes in cleaning quality, drainage, or water control. If you notice any of the following, it may be time to schedule service.

01

Not draining properly

If water remains at the bottom after a cycle, the issue may involve the drain system, pump, filter, hose, or another internal drainage-related component.

02

Dishes coming out dirty

Poor cleaning results can be caused by spray arm issues, water circulation problems, filter blockage, heating-related problems, or incorrect wash performance.

03

Leaking water

If the dishwasher leaks during or after a cycle, the issue may involve seals, hoses, connections, alignment, or water management inside the unit.

04

Dishwasher not starting

If the unit does not respond when you start a cycle, the issue may involve power delivery, latch-related problems, controls, or internal electrical components.

05

Unusual sounds during operation

Grinding, buzzing, or repeated abnormal sounds can point to pump strain, blockage, moving component issues, or another internal malfunction that should be inspected.

06

Unit runs but performs inconsistently

Some dishwashers complete a cycle but fail to wash, drain, or dry properly. This can point to control issues, heating problems, or component wear that affects overall performance.
